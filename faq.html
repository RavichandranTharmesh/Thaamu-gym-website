Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the membership options available?

We offer a variety of membership options to suit your needs, including monthly, quarterly, and annual memberships. We also provide special discounts for students and corporate groups.

What are the gym's operating hours?

Our gym is open from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM from Monday to Friday, and from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekends. This allows you to choose a time that best fits your schedule.

What classes do you offer?

We offer a wide range of classes including yoga, Pilates, Zumba, spinning, and strength training. Our certified instructors ensure a great workout experience for all fitness levels.

Do you have personal trainers available?

Yes, we have certified personal trainers who can create a customized workout plan to help you achieve your fitness goals. Personal training sessions are available by appointment.

What facilities do you offer?

Our gym is equipped with the latest fitness machines, free weights, cardio equipment, and a dedicated stretching area. We also have locker rooms, showers, and a sauna for your convenience.

What are your health and safety measures?

Your health and safety are our top priority. We have implemented enhanced cleaning protocols, hand sanitizing stations throughout the gym, and social distancing measures in all workout areas.
